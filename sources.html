Referenced Works & Citations

Primary Theoretical Frameworks

  1. Einstein-Cartan Theory: Élie Cartan (1929), "Sur les variétés à connexion affine et la théorie de la relativité généralisée" — Foundation for torsion in spacetime geometry
  2. Friedmann Equations: Alexander Friedmann (1922), "Über die Krümmung des Raumes" — Standard cosmological evolution equations, here modified with torsion terms
  3. Loop Quantum Gravity Bounces: Abhay Ashtekar et al. (2006-2024), foundational work on quantum bounce mechanisms
  4. String Gas Cosmology: Robert Brandenberger et al. (2000s), alternative bounce framework

Note: The specific form of torsion-corrected Friedmann equations with spin-density coupling (H² = (8πG/3)ρ - k·σ²) represents a novel contribution by Houston Golden. The underlying Einstein-Cartan framework and Friedmann formalism are established prior work.

Observational Data Sources

  1. DESI BAO Measurements (2024-2025): Dark Energy Spectroscopic Instrument collaboration, baryon acoustic oscillation constraints — strongest current limits on bounce energy scale
  2. Planck CMB Data (2018 release): Cosmic microwave background power spectra, constraining early universe physics
  3. Pantheon+ SNe Ia (2022): Type Ia supernovae distance measurements for late-time cosmology validation
