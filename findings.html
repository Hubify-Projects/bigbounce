Torsion-Mediated Big Bounce

Spin-torsion coupling in Einstein-Cartan theory provides a natural mechanism for cosmological bounce, avoiding the initial singularity of standard Big Bang cosmology. Critical density ρ_c = 3k·σ²/(8πG) acts as the bounce threshold.
