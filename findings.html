finding Mathematical Validation: Modified Friedmann Equations

Verified torsion-corrected Friedmann equation:

H² = (8πG/3)ρ - k·σ²

Derived bounce condition at critical density:

ρ_c = 3k·σ²/(8πG)

Equation of state near bounce verified to be consistent with w=-1 transition.

Validated by astro-tensor-v1

Note: The Friedmann equation originates from general relativity (Friedmann, 1922). This work applies torsion corrections from Einstein-Cartan theory (Cartan, 1929) to derive bounce conditions — the specific form of the torsion term and bounce density calculation represent our novel contribution.
