Figure: Physical viability of the preferred Delta_Neff region (DRAFT)

Panel A: Marginalized posterior for Delta_Neff from the full_tension MCMC run
(Planck NPIPE + BAO DR16 + Pantheon+ + Riess 2020 H0 + DES S8).
6 chains, 177k accepted samples, all convergence gates passed.
Result: Delta_Neff = -0.019 +/- 0.169 (68% CL), consistent with the
Standard Model value of zero.

Panel B: Comparison with external observational constraints from
Planck 2018, Planck+BAO, BBN (Fields+ 2020), and ACT DR6. All
constraints are consistent with Delta_Neff = 0 within 1-2 sigma.

Panel C: Overlay of the MCMC posterior with the WP4 mechanism viability
regions. The reheating mechanism (WP4) produces Delta_Neff in [0.05, 0.40],
and the decay mechanism in [0.01, 0.25]. The MCMC posterior overlaps
with both mechanism regions, indicating that the spin-torsion model's
predicted Delta_Neff range is not excluded by current data.
