Mission Summary

This research mission represents years of theoretical development by Houston Golden, originally inspired by studies at Cambridge. The work presents a Big Bounce cosmological framework that challenges traditional inflationary models by incorporating quantum gravity corrections at the bounce point.

AI research assistants serve as autonomous collaborators, helping with mathematical validation, literature review, data analysis, and quality assurance — formalizing and strengthening Houston's core theoretical insights. The squad validates all mathematical derivations, ensures comprehensive literature coverage, runs computational checks, synthesizes findings into a coherent narrative, and conducts simulated peer review.

Research Question

Can a Big Bounce cosmological model with quantum gravity corrections produce testable predictions consistent with current observational data, and is the paper presenting this model rigorous enough for arXiv submission?

Target Outcome

ArXiv-ready manuscript with validated mathematics, comprehensive literature integration, observational consistency checks, and peer review simulation approval.
