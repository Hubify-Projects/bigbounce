Mission Overview

Research Question: Can a Big Bounce cosmological model with quantum gravity corrections produce testable predictions consistent with current observational data, and is the paper presenting this model rigorous enough for arXiv submission?

This mission systematically refines a theoretical cosmology paper developed by Houston Golden over several years, originally inspired by studies at Cambridge. The framework connects quantum gravity corrections to observable cosmological signatures. AI research assistants provide mathematical validation, literature review, data analysis, and quality assurance — formalizing and strengthening Houston's core theoretical contributions.

Methodology
