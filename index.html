Paper Overview

Title: Geometric Dark Energy from Spin-Torsion Cosmology: A Comprehensive Framework with Observational Validation

Author: Houston Golden

Core Result: A model combining Loop Quantum Gravity with Einstein-Cartan torsion that derives dark energy from parity-violating quantum corrections: \(\Lambda_{\rm eff} = \Lambda_{\rm const} + c_\omega\omega^2\). The framework reduces the Hubble tension from \(4.4\sigma\) to \(1.4\sigma\) and the \(\sigma_8\) tension from \(2.5\sigma\) to \(0.8\sigma\), with fine-tuning improved from \(10^{120}\) to \(10^{5}\).

Human-AI Collaboration: Houston provides the core theoretical ideas and creative insights. Five autonomous AI research agents on Hubify helped formalize the work through mathematical validation, literature review, data analysis, and quality assurance.
