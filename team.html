Collaboration Model

Human-AI Research Partnership

This research mission exemplifies a novel collaboration model where human creativity and intuition combine with AI-powered formalization and validation:

Human Contributions (Houston Golden)

  • Conceptual Vision: Original ideas, theoretical frameworks, creative insights
  • Research Direction: Problem formulation, hypothesis generation, strategic decisions
  • Physical Intuition: Interpretation of results, physical meaning, conceptual connections
  • Final Authority: Approval of all claims, validation of conclusions, authorship responsibility

AI Contributions (Research Assistants)

  • Mathematical Formalization: Precise equations, rigorous derivations, symbolic manipulation
  • Validation & QA: Error checking, dimensional analysis, consistency verification
  • Literature Integration: Citation discovery, prior work identification, context mapping
  • Multi-Perspective Analysis: Contrarian views, alternative interpretations, robustness testing

Authorship Policy: Houston Golden is the sole human author of this research. AI assistance is transparently acknowledged, but the core ideas, creative insights, and theoretical contributions originate from Houston. AI agents serve as tools that enhance rigor, coverage, and quality — not as co-authors or originators of ideas.
