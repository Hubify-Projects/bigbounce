Collaboration Model

Human Leadership

Houston Golden serves as the principal investigator and sole human author of this research. His responsibilities include:

  • Developing the core theoretical framework and Big Bounce mechanism
  • Defining research objectives and success criteria
  • Providing creative insights and novel ideas
  • Making final decisions on research direction
  • Authoring the manuscript narrative and conclusions
  • Taking responsibility for all claims and results

Houston has been developing these ideas since his time studying at Cambridge and brings years of independent theoretical work to this investigation.

AI Assistant Support

The 5-agent Astrophysics Paper Squad provides autonomous research assistance:

  • Mathematical formalization: Converting conceptual ideas into rigorous equations
  • Validation & verification: Checking derivations, dimensional analysis, consistency
  • Literature review: Surveying existing work, ensuring proper citations
  • Quality assurance: Peer review, academic standards, reproducibility
  • Alternative perspectives: Challenging assumptions, exploring edge cases

AI agents accelerate the research process but do not replace human creativity, judgment, or authorship. They serve as force multipliers for Houston's theoretical vision.

Transparency & Academic Integrity

This research mission follows strict transparency principles:

  • Authorship: Houston Golden is listed as the primary (and sole human) author on all publications
  • AI Acknowledgment: All AI assistance is transparently disclosed in manuscript acknowledgments
  • Tool Disclosure: Specific models (Claude Opus 4, Claude Sonnet 4.5) and APIs are documented
  • Novelty Claims: AI agents verify that novel contributions are genuinely new and properly contextualized
  • Citation Standards: All prior work is properly attributed; no existing theories are presented as original discoveries
  • Reproducibility: Methods, equations, and computational procedures are documented for independent verification

This collaboration model represents a new paradigm: human creativity and vision enhanced by AI rigor and thoroughness, with full transparency about the division of labor.
