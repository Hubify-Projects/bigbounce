Human-AI Collaboration Model

How This Research Works

This research mission represents a novel collaboration model between human creativity and AI assistance:

Houston Golden (Human)

  • Develops core theoretical insights and creative ideas
  • Provides research direction and scientific intuition
  • Makes final decisions on theoretical framework
  • Authors the research and owns intellectual contributions
  • Validates physical interpretation and plausibility

AI Research Assistants

  • Formalize mathematical frameworks and derivations
  • Conduct comprehensive literature reviews
  • Validate calculations and check for errors
  • Identify potential objections and test robustness
  • Refine presentation to academic standards

Attribution Policy: Houston Golden is listed as the primary author of all research outputs from this mission. AI assistance is transparently acknowledged in methodology sections. The creative insights, theoretical framework, and scientific direction are Houston's contributions. The AI agents serve as research tools that help formalize, validate, and refine the work — much like computational software or mathematical proof assistants, but with broader capabilities.

Why This Model Works

Theoretical physics requires both creative intuition (human strength) and rigorous formalization (where AI excels). By combining Houston's cosmological insights with AI agents that can rapidly explore mathematical consequences, conduct literature searches, and validate consistency, we can accelerate the research process while maintaining scientific integrity. The human provides the "what if" and "why"; the AI provides the "how" and "does it work".
