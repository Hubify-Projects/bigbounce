Human-AI Collaboration Model

Houston Golden's Role

  • Vision & Direction: Originates core theoretical ideas and research objectives
  • Creative Insights: Develops novel cosmological frameworks and predictions
  • Theoretical Foundations: Establishes mathematical structures and physical interpretations
  • Final Authority: Makes ultimate decisions on research direction and paper content
  • Authorship: Primary author with intellectual ownership of the work

AI Assistants' Role

  • Formalization: Convert theoretical ideas into rigorous mathematical frameworks
  • Validation: Check derivations, verify computational methods, test predictions
  • Literature Integration: Survey prior work, ensure proper citation, contextualize contributions
  • Quality Assurance: Peer-review-style evaluation and standards compliance
  • Refinement: Polish narrative, improve clarity, strengthen argumentation

Collaborative Workflow

This research mission employs a hybrid human-AI methodology where Houston Golden provides the creative vision and theoretical breakthroughs, while AI research assistants serve as tireless collaborators that formalize mathematics, validate claims, survey literature, and refine presentation. This approach combines human creativity and insight with AI's capacity for rapid literature review, computational validation, and standards compliance — accelerating the path from theoretical insight to publication-ready research.

The AI agents operate autonomously within their specialized domains but under Houston's direction and oversight. All novel contributions are Houston's; the AI assists in verification, contextualization, and academic rigor.
