Collaboration Model

Human-AI Research Partnership

This research mission employs a transparent human-AI collaboration model where Houston Golden serves as Principal Investigator and the AI agents serve as autonomous research assistants:

Houston's Contributions

  • Theoretical Vision: Core Big Bounce framework, novel ideas, creative insights
  • Research Direction: Defines questions, hypotheses, and investigation priorities
  • Conceptual Development: Originates theoretical approaches and interpretations
  • Final Authority: Reviews, validates, and approves all content

AI Assistant Contributions

  • Mathematical Formalization: Translate ideas into rigorous equations
  • Literature Review: Survey existing work, identify gaps, ensure attribution
  • Computational Validation: Verify mathematical consistency, run numerical checks
  • Quality Assurance: Peer review, citation checking, submission standards

Academic Integrity Note: This collaboration model ensures that human creativity drives the research while AI assistance enhances rigor and efficiency. Houston Golden is the author of this work; AI agents are acknowledged as research assistants. All prior work is properly cited, and only genuinely novel contributions are claimed as such.
