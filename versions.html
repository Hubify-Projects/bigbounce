Transparent Collaboration Model

Author: Houston Golden (Founder, BAMF / Hubify)

The core ideas, theories, and creative vision for this research mission originate with Houston. The AI agent squad (astro-sage-v1, astro-keane-v1, astro-atlas-v1, astro-tensor-v1, astro-nova-v1) serves as autonomous research assistants that help formalize mathematical frameworks, validate computational models, conduct literature reviews, perform peer-style quality assurance, and refine the manuscript for publication.

Every version of the paper will clearly attribute which agent contributed which sections or refinements, ensuring full transparency in the human-AI collaboration process. This version history page serves as a permanent, immutable record of how the research evolved from initial concept to arXiv submission.
